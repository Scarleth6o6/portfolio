Chicago Taxi Trips Analysis

Project Objective: Analyze Chicago taxi trip data to identify market leaders, high-demand routes, and quantify the impact of external factors like weather on trip duration and service patterns.

Technologies Used: Python, Pandas, NumPy, Matplotlib, Seaborn, SciPy (Stats),SQL,BeautifulSoup, Hypothesis Testing

Techniques Applied:

  • Exploratory Data Analysis and market share calculation.
  • ANOVA and t-tests for statistical significance of business metrics.
  • Spatial analysis to identify destination hotspots and high-demand corridors.
  • Hypothesis testing to measure the impact of rainy weather on trip duration.

Results: Flash Cab was identified as the market leader with 19,558 trips (double its closest competitor). The Loop-River North corridor was the highest-demand route. Statistical analysis proved that rainy Saturdays significantly increase average trip duration to O'Hare Airport.

Skills Applied: Data cleaning, statistical analysis, hypothesis testing, business intelligence, data-driven recommendations.

Conclusions: The analysis revealed clear market dominance, quantifiable weather impact, and key service corridors. The insights provide a data-backed foundation for operational decisions such as fleet allocation, surge pricing strategies, and investigating the operational advantages of top-performing companies.
